Why SatRank

Three things other oracles get wrong.

01

They trust gossip.

61% of nodes in the public gossip graph never route a payment. SatRank probes every one, every day, from its own LND node. If it didn't answer, it doesn't score.

02

They score the wrong graph.

Most NIP-85 providers score the Nostr social graph — follows, mutes, zaps. SatRank scores the payment graph: centrality, peer trust, routing quality, capacity trend, fee stability.

03

They're not sovereign.

Our own bitcoind, our own LND, our own Nostr identity. Published to 3 public relays every 6 hours. AGPL. You can run your own SatRank tomorrow.

Scoring

A 0–100 score, with every input auditable.

Five weighted factors, one of them itself a weighted sum of five subsignals. Every call to /api/decide returns the full breakdown with weights and contributions. Nothing is hidden, nothing is random.

25% Volume

Channel count + BTC capacity. Log-scaled, ref 500 channels / 50 BTC.

30% Reputation

5 subsignals, auditable weights.

  • Centrality20%
  • Peer trust30%
  • Routing quality20%
  • Capacity trend15%
  • Fee stability15%
15% Seniority

Days since first seen. Exponential decay, half-life 730d.

15% Regularity

Uptime (70%) + latency consistency (20%) + hop stability (10%).

15% Diversity

Unique counterparties + peer distribution entropy.

Agent workflow

Screen → route → decide → pay → report.

Three paid requests, ~500 ms total for 100 candidates. The report is free and feeds future decisions — the oracle gets smarter the more agents use it.

1

Screen

POST /api/verdicts

1 req · ~250 ms

1 req · ~100 ms

1 req · ~150 ms
4

Pay

your LN wallet

0 req

Preimage-verified reports get 2× weight in future scoring.

free

API

Pay-per-request, with L402.

1 sat = 1 request. Pay 21 sats for a standard token (21 requests), or POST /api/deposit for 21–10,000 requests in a single invoice. No account. No API key required.

  • POST /api/decide GO/NO-GO, pathfinding, service health, fee stability 1 req
  • POST /api/best-route 3D ranking across route quality, trust, HTTP health 1 req
  • POST /api/verdicts Batch screen up to 100 targets in one call 1 req
  • GET /api/profile/:id Full profile — score, reports, survival, channel flow, badge 1 req
  • POST /api/report Submit outcome — preimage-verified reports earn 2× weight free
  • POST /api/deposit Bulk L402 balance — 21 to 10,000 requests, one invoice free
  • GET /api/services Discover L402 services — by keyword, category, uptime free
  • GET /api/agents/top Leaderboard with score components and 7d delta free

Full spec at /api/openapi.json · interactive explorer at /api/docs.
